Build ecom sites, super fast

This template was built to help you rapidly, and modularly, build out high quality e-commerce concepts using pre-built content blocks. Customise and play around with the template to match your specific requirements, and most of all have fun with it!

Use the styles

Styles in Figma mean you don’t have to repetitively enter values for typography, colors and elevations, minimising the risk of inconsistencies and streamlining the design process.

Use the components

There’s a bunch of pre-configured components for the Style Guide page and under the components section. Buttons, steppers, filter options, and more for your tastes and requirements.
